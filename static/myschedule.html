MySchedule

This page will contain the MySchedule app.

Here's how it works:

  1. Add this weeks upcoming to do's and their due dats for each course (homework, exams, chapters to read, concepts to study, etc)
  2. Schedule each to do item for a day you intend to do it.
  3. Bang it out and mark items as "completed" when you get them done.
  4. You will be notified for items that are due TODAY, and for items that are PAST DUE.
  5. Yes, this is a glorified to do list with a specific application.
